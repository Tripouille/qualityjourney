# Product Vision

## Mission

QualityJourney.dev is the reference platform for Modern QA Engineers.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Home
Marketing landing page promoting the "Modern QA" vision.

**Purpose:**
- Attract QA professionals seeking modern skills
- Showcase the platform's value proposition
- Drive conversions to course enrollment

### 2. Courses
Training catalog for certification and skill development.

**Initial Focus:**
- ISTQB Foundation Level (primary)
- Future: Playwright, Cypress, API Testing, Performance Testing

**Course Structure:**
- Chapters → Sections → Lessons → Quizzes
- Progress tracking
- Certificate upon completion

### 3. Blog
SEO-focused content hub.

**Purpose:**
- Organic traffic acquisition
- Thought leadership
- Community engagement

**Status:** Placeholder (future implementation)

### 4. Public Profile
User page with gamification elements.

**Features:**
- Earned certificates display
- Progress heatmap (daily activity)
- Course completion stats
- Shareable public URL

## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: Aspiring QA Engineer

**Profile:**
- 2-5 years in software testing
- Wants ISTQB certification
- Seeks structured learning path
- Values practical, exam-focused content

**Pain Points:**
- ISTQB syllabus is dense and hard to study alone
- Existing courses lack comprehensive quizzes
- No clear indication of exam readiness
- Generic materials don't mirror real exam format

**What They Need:**
- Exhaustive quiz practice (100% coverage)
- Detailed explanations for every question
- Mobile-friendly study experience
- Clear progress tracking

### Secondary Persona: Self-Taught Tester

**Profile:**
- Transitioned from manual testing or dev
- No formal QA education
- Needs foundational knowledge
- Prefers self-paced learning

**Pain Points:**
- Gaps in fundamental testing knowledge
- Don't know what they don't know
- Need structured curriculum

**What They Need:**
- Comprehensive coverage of testing fundamentals
- Clear learning objectives
- Mastery-based progression (can't skip ahead)

---

## Value Proposition

### What Sets Us Apart

**1. Exhaustive, Not Superficial**
- Every ISTQB Learning Objective gets a question
- No arbitrary quiz limits (e.g., "5 questions per section")
- Mastery over speed

**2. Exam Fidelity**
- Questions mirror real ISTQB exam structure
- Uses exact syllabus terminology
- References syllabus sections in explanations

**3. Satisfying UX**
- Confetti celebrations on quiz pass
- Detailed explanations for every answer
- Beautiful, mobile-first reading experience

**4. 100% Pass Requirement**
- Sections unlock only after 100% quiz score
- Ensures deep understanding, not just checkbox clicking
- Unlimited retakes with detailed feedback

**5. No Video Bloat**
- All content is structured text + interactive components
- Faster to consume
- Easier to maintain and update
- More accessible (translation, search, screen readers)

## Brand Voice

**Modern, Not Stuffy**
- Clear, direct language
- No corporate jargon
- Approachable tone

**Mastery-Focused**
- Emphasize deep learning over quick wins
- Celebrate milestones meaningfully
- Respect the user's learning journey

**Mobile-First Mindset**
- Acknowledge users study on-the-go
- Design for distraction-free focus
- Prioritize readability and accessibility

---

## Non-Goals (What We're NOT Building)

❌ Video-based courses (text + interactive components only)
❌ Live instructor-led training
❌ Job board or recruiting platform
❌ Social network features (keeping it focused on learning)
❌ Freemium with locked content (TBD business model)
